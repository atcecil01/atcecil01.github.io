About The Project

I started out in my dad's woodshop, but quickly progressed to building complex projects with intricate details. This teabox was an anniversary gift for my wife, and features 4 separate compartments to hold tea bags.

This project site is intended to show projects I have built. If you have any questions about any projects seen here, or would like blueprints for them feel free to contact me. Make sure to use proper safety equipment when working.
